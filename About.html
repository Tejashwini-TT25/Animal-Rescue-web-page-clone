About Us
There are total of 5707 animals shelters in India of 2023 & many NGOs present in India which are protecting the animals, But even in the presence of these we can find many animals in the streets in crucial conditions that have been neglected, abused, or ignored by society. So we are providing a chance, a platform for the people and society to protect these animals to help them restore their lives for their wellbeing.
